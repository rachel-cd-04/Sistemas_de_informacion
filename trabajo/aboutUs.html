Who are we?

TFT Manual is a project developed by Raquel Cámara Domene y Mateo Peña Costa, a group of students from the University of Zaragoza at Spain. The project was developed as part of the course "Introduction to Coumputer Science" from Harvard during the Summer of 2024. The purpose of the project is to provide a tool for players of the game Teamfight Tactics to manage their teams and make de perfect comp in the new Set 12. The project was developed using HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
